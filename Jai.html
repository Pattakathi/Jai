Early Cancer Prediction and Analysis Using AI/ML & DP for Personalized Medicine and Precision Drug Delivery System.

Synthetic Antibody Designing & Predictive Analysis of Ab-Ag Interaction and Computational Modeling.

This project uses AI, ML, and DL to develop bioinformatics tools for early cancer detection using medical images. It designs personalized cancer treatments, including synthetic antibodies for immunotherapy and precision drug delivery via liposomes and dendrimers, minimizing side effects.
